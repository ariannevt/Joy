Why Joy?

Happiness is different for everyone. In this project, I asked my college friends: "What is your main source of joy?" Their answers showed how small moments, deep connections, and personal passions create joy in everyday life.

This project, part of my Communications Lab course, was inspired by the movie Inside Out, a film that helped children understand their emotions by introducing Joy, Sadness, Anger, Fear, and Disgustâ€”each representing a fundamental part of how we feel. Just like in the movie, this project explores how emotions shape our lives and connections.
