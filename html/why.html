Why Joy?

Happiness is a universal yet deeply personal experience. What brings joy to one person may not be the same for another. This project was born out of curiosity— what truly makes people happy? I asked my college friends, “What is your main source of joy?” and their responses highlighted the power of small moments, deep connections, and personal passions in shaping everyday happiness.

Inspired by Inside Out, a film that helped children understand their emotions through characters like Joy, Sadness, Anger, Fear, and Disgust, this project explores how digital spaces can capture emotions, memories, and perspectives in meaningful ways. Just like in the movie, where Joy learns that emotions are complex and interconnected, this page is a celebration of the moments that bring us happiness, big or small.

So, what brings you joy?
